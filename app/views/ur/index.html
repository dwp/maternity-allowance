  • Number of participants: 5
  • Overall this was well received by agents. They understood that this was a service that was intended for their use, just not exactly what it would do.
  • There were no issues with the data capture.
  • There was an expectation that this information would be saved if they needed to leave the claim or BF it at any point.
  • Some agents did notice that the date last worked was not shown on the summary page.
  • Agents did not understand where the information on the summary page had come from to form the decision.
  • However, it did seem like there was a greater level of trust in this information than there was during the previous scenario-based research.
  • The MAP was something agents expected to see as part of this decision, or as the next step in the process.
  • Some future areas to consider: call to action, date last worked, MAP, earnings test.
